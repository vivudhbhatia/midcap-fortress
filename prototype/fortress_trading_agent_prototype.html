About

This is a frontend-only demo. It uses synthetic price series to show: (1) time-window summaries & drill-down, (2) full run ledger with hashes, (3) governance of parameter changes, and (4) a multi-strategy switching framework aligned to the goal of >15% CAGR over long horizons with reduced risk.
Not investment advice. Real data, brokerage execution, and production controls require a backend service.
